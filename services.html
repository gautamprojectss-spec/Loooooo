Service FAQs

Questions before you choose a service.

What services does Colours & Patterns offer?

We offer brand strategy and identity, social media marketing, performance advertising, website development, verified WhatsApp API marketing, and content and creative production.

Do you build websites?

Yes. We design and develop conversion-ready websites with responsive layouts, SEO-friendly architecture, lead-capture forms, and WhatsApp or CRM integrations.

Do you offer WhatsApp marketing services?

Yes. We provide verified WhatsApp Business API marketing, including campaign broadcasts, CRM-integrated flows, automated welcome journeys, and lead routing.

How does your social media process work?

We plan content in advance, build a monthly calendar, create posts and reels, manage approvals, publish consistently, and report performance each month.

Can you combine services into one plan?

Yes. Many clients combine social media, ads, websites, WhatsApp campaigns, and creative production into one integrated monthly service plan.
